A Brief History of Coffee

Coffee is a brewed drink prepared from roasted coffee beans, which are the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant.

Origins and Spread

Legend has it that coffee was discovered by a goat herder in Ethiopia, who noticed his goats became energetic after eating berries from a certain tree. From there, coffee cultivation spread to the Arabian Peninsula.

Key Historical Moments

  1. 9th Century: Discovery in Ethiopia.
  2. 15th Century: Cultivation in Yemen.
  3. 17th Century: Arrival in Europe.

Popular Coffee Types

  • Espresso
  • Latte
  • Cappuccino
